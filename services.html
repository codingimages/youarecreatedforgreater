It's about you

At Created for Greater Psychiatric Rehabilitation Program we provide a wide-range of support services to children, youth and adults. The offered services are designed to promote wellness, self sufficiency and independent living skills amongst others.

Our plan is to serve as many people in the community as possible. We service children and youth at least three times a month and adults at least six times a month. Customized programs available.

During visits, psychiatric rehabilitation counselors will assist clients with achieving their rehabilitation goals by providing an array of tools and services to our clients. Some of those services are providing psycho education to assist clients with understanding their diagnoses, assistance with either medication management, symptom/illness management, education on proper nutrition, assistance with maintaining personal hygiene, money management, social skills, conflict resolution skills, communication skills, anger management skills, vocational skills, coping skills and many more. Your wellnes can't wait and we are here to really help!
