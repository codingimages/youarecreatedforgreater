About us

Our mission statement

You are in the center!

The mission of Created for Greater Psychiatric Rehabilitation Program is to improve the quality of life for the residents of Maryland by educating, elevating and empowering them to their highest level of functioning. Our mission is about helping you.

OUR FULL SERVICES

It's about you

Our primary purpose is to serve individuals with emotional, psychological, psychiatric and behavioral deficits and provide high quality services to empower individuals to achieve personal success in their lives. Our team benefit the community in many ways by providing needed and timely access to services and treatments. We strive to utilize treatments with the best evidence of effectiveness. We will treat all clients with dignity and respect and hold their confidentiality up to the highest of standards. We understand every case is unique, that's why we are committed to providing the highest quality mental health services by providing individualized interventions that are designed to revive, restore, rehabilitate and renew all of our clients.
